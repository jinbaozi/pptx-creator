# toc

This archetype defines a table-of-contents slide that lists section titles with optional page anchors. It should be selected by `slide-design-specs.json` through the `layoutType` field when the slide role is "navigation" or "agenda".

Use this layout when the deck needs an early orientation slide that previews the upcoming section flow without becoming a duplicate of the cover. Keep entries short, mutually exclusive, and reflective of the actual section titles.
