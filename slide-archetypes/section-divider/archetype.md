# section-divider

This archetype defines a large section title with an optional section number, used to mark a transition between major parts of a deck. It should be selected by `slide-design-specs.json` through the `layoutType` field when the slide role is "section break".

Use this layout sparingly — once per major section. Keep the divider visually quiet relative to the cover; the divider exists to signal a pause, not to compete for attention.
