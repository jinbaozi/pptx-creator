# quote

This archetype defines a large pull-quote slide with attribution. It should be selected by `slide-design-specs.json` through the `layoutType` field when the slide role is "testimonial", "key claim", or "evidence highlight".

Use this layout when one statement deserves the full slide. Pair with attribution that names the speaker (or source) and their role. Do not use this layout for ordinary claims that fit a bullets-list.
